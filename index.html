About me

Moin,

I’m Mae, originally from the beautiful city of Hamburg, and I’ve been fully immersed in the world of game and software development for the past few years. Whether as a hobby or professionally – I genuinely enjoy creating new worlds and finding creative solutions to complex challenges.

My main tools are Unity and C# – they’re my favorite combo when it comes to building interactive experiences. I also have a solid background in 3D modeling, mainly using Blender to bring the visual side of my projects to life. On top of that, I have a strong passion for 2D art, which I love incorporating into my games and other creative work.

Aside from working on personal projects – many of which come to life during game jams – I’m currently the lead developer at Aposan, where I’m working on a point-and-click serious game. I really enjoy being part of projects that are not only fun to play, but also provide real value – especially when developed together with a great team.
